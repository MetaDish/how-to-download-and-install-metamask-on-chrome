Installation Guide

Follow these simple steps to install and set up MetaMask

1

Visit the Official Website

Go to metamask.io and click "Download" to ensure you're getting the official extension. Never download MetaMask from third-party sources.

Security Tip

Always verify the URL is exactly "metamask.io" to avoid phishing sites

2

Install the Extension

Click "Install MetaMask for [Your Browser]" and follow your browser's prompts to add the extension. The fox icon will appear in your browser toolbar.

Chrome Add-ons Firefox Add-ons Brave Add-ons Opera Add-ons Edge Add-ons
3

Create Your Wallet

Click the MetaMask icon and select "Create a new wallet". You'll be asked to create a password for accessing your wallet on this device.

  • Choose a strong, unique password
  • Store it securely - you'll need it every time
  • Never share your password with anyone
4

Secure Your Recovery Phrase

MetaMask will show you a 12-word Secret Recovery Phrase. This is the MOST IMPORTANT step - write it down and store it somewhere safe offline.

Critical Warning

Anyone with your recovery phrase can access your funds. Never share it, never store it digitally, and never enter it on any website.

5

Confirm and Start Using

Verify your recovery phrase by selecting the words in the correct order. Once confirmed, your wallet is ready to use!
